    Who is a Campus Ambassador?

    Campus Ambassadors are an integral part of the eDC who work closely with the eDC and help us reach their campus students. They are a part of the team that plays a significant role in managing events, workshops, eDC social media pages, and so much more! They are students who will be the voice of eDC on their campus, encouraging their fellow students to become more involved in entrepreneurship.

    The motive for CAP

    Make a community of young entrepreneurial minds in Delhi NCR and all over India Bringing together young minds | sparking conversations and new ventures Outreach and make a brand name for eDC.

    This opportunity is only open for students of colleges apart from IIT DELHI. IITD students will get an opportunity to volunteer BECon later.
